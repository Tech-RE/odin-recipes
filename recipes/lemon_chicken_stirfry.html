Lemon Chicken

Description

Who needs takeaways when you can make this low-fat Chinese-style stir-fry in half an hour?

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp honey
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 150ml chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp spy sauce
  • 4 chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 6 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 carrots, finiely sliced
  • 1 red pepper, cut into chunks
  • 140g sugar snap peas

Steps

    Heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan, then fry the chicken until it changes colour and starts to become crisp around the edges.
  2. Add the carrots and red pepper, then fry for 1 min more. Pour the stock into the pan, bring to a simmer,
    then add the sugar snap peas and bubble everything together for 5-10 mins until the chicken is cooked and the veg are tender. Serve with noodles.
